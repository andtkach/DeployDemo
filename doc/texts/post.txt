Just published a practical demo on Kubernetes deployment strategies for developers.

A lot of devs I talk to in interviews are solid on code but get uncomfortable when the conversation shifts to deployments. So I put together a hands-on walkthrough covering the three strategies you actually see in production: Rolling Update, Canary, and Blue-Green.

The demo uses a small distributed app (React UI, Node.js API, MongoDB, background processor) running in a local Kubernetes cluster. Everything from scratch — no shortcuts.

Published in three languages:

English: [ https://youtu.be/A2FA7LMlsDo ]
Ukrainian: [ https://youtu.be/Kq28gwWA3UA ]
Spanish: [ https://youtu.be/xHmAj7LvXzQ ]

GitHub: [ https://github.com/andtkach/DeployDemo ]

If you are a developer who wants to understand what happens after coding — this one is for you.
